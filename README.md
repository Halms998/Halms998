## 🔭 What I'm Working On

- Exploring the **full AI pipeline** — from data to deployment to real-world impact
- Asking the questions tech CEOs won't: *Is the bubble real? Will hand-coders
  make a comeback? What does AI actually change, and what does it just rename?*
- Building things. Breaking things. Understanding why they broke.

---

## 🧠 What I'm Thinking About

I'm genuinely fascinated by the **boundary between AI and human consciousness** —
where the line is, whether it's blurring, and whether it even matters in the long run.

We're training LLMs on human thought. But what's the reverse effect?
Are humans getting sharper to keep up, or quietly getting outsourced?
It's part psychology, part philosophy, part something we don't have a name for yet.

I don't have the answers. But I'm paying close attention.
